💼 面试场景 Interview Scenario

Practice common interview questions and answers

❓ Tell me about yourself ▼

回答结构：当前职位 → 相关经验 → 为什么感兴趣

Example:

"I'm currently a software engineer with 5 years of experience in cloud computing. I specialize in microservices architecture and have led several successful migration projects. I'm interested in this role because it offers opportunities to work with cutting-edge technologies."

❓ What are your strengths? ▼

常用表达：

  • "One of my key strengths is..."
  • "I'm particularly good at..."
  • "I excel in..."
❓ What are your weaknesses? ▼

回答策略：选择真实但不致命的弱点 + 说明如何改进

Example:

"I used to struggle with public speaking, but I've been actively working on it by volunteering to present in team meetings. I've seen significant improvement and now feel much more confident."

❓ Describe a challenging project ▼

使用STAR方法：

  • Situation - What was the context?
  • Task - What was your responsibility?
  • Action - What did you do?
  • Result - What was the outcome?

🏃 站会场景 Stand-up Meeting

Practice daily stand-up meeting updates

1️⃣ What did I do yesterday?

Template:

"Yesterday, I [completed/worked on] [specific task]. I also [additional accomplishment]."

常用动词：Completed, Implemented, Fixed, Reviewed, Deployed, Collaborated with

2️⃣ What will I do today?

Template:

"Today, I plan to [main task]. I'll also [secondary task] if time permits."

常用表达：

  • "I'll start working on..."
  • "My priority today is..."
  • "I plan to complete..."

3️⃣ Are there any blockers?

如果没有："No blockers at the moment." / "Everything is on track."

如果有："I'm blocked by [specific issue]. I need [what you need] to move forward."

常见阻碍：Waiting for code review, Need access to environment, Dependency on another team

✅ 站会最佳实践

✅ DO
  • Be Prepared
  • Be Concise (1-2 min)
  • Be Specific
  • Speak Clearly
❌ DON'T
  • Go Into Details
  • Problem-Solve
  • Interrupt
  • Be Late

🎉 破冰活动 Ice-Breaking

Build confidence with fun introduction activities

自我介绍模板 Self-Introduction

Hi, I'm [Your Name].

I work in [Department/Team].

An interesting fact about me is [something unique].

I'm here today to [your learning goal].

两个真相一个谎言 Two Truths and a Lie

说三句关于自己的话，其中两句是真的，一句是假的，让其他人猜哪句是假的。

Example:

  1. I've been to 10 countries.
  2. I can speak three languages.
  3. I've climbed Mount Everest.
